About Purely Canadian Movers

Purely Canadian Movers is a family-owned moving company founded in 1991 and based in Coquitlam, British Columbia. For more than three decades we have moved households and businesses across Metro Vancouver, Canada, and to the USA — using our own trained crews rather than subcontractors, so accountability stays with us from the first estimate to final delivery.
