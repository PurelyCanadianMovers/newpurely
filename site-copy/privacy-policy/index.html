Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Purely Canadian Movers Inc. respects your privacy. This page explains how we handle information submitted through our website, quote forms, phone calls, emails, and related customer communications.

Information we collect

When you request an estimate or contact us, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, moving origin and destination, move date, home or office size, inventory notes, access details, and any additional information you choose to provide.

How we use information

  • To respond to moving estimate requests and service questions.
  • To plan local, long-distance, packing, storage, or office moving services.
  • To communicate about scheduling, access, valuation coverage, claims, and customer support.
  • To improve website performance, search visibility, and customer experience.

Analytics and website data

We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use the website. This can include pages viewed, approximate location, device type, browser type, referral source, and general interaction data. Analytics data is used to improve the site and is not used to sell personal information.

Sharing information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when needed to provide moving services, respond to your request, comply with legal obligations, protect our business, or work with trusted service providers who support our website or customer communications.

Contact us about privacy

For questions about this policy or your information, contact Purely Canadian Movers at esales@pcmovers.ca or call 1-877-485-6683.
