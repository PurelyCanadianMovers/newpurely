Common questions

What is your moving network?

Purely Canadian Movers coordinates long-distance moves through direct customer accountability and the Great Canadian Van Lines agent network.

Are you a moving broker?

No. Purely Canadian Movers is not a moving broker. Long-distance moves are coordinated through Purely Canadian Movers and the Great Canadian Van Lines agent network.

Why does the network matter?

A coordinated agent network helps with long-distance route planning, linehaul, storage timing, delivery support, and documentation.

Can the network support cross-country moves?

Yes. The network supports long-distance and cross-country moves across Canada.
