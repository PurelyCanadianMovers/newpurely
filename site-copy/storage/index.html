Common questions

Do you offer storage with moving services?

Yes. Storage can be included with local moves, long-distance moves, office moves, and moves where pickup and delivery dates do not line up.

Can my items be packed for storage?

Yes. Packing can be coordinated so items are prepared for storage, handling, and later delivery.

Is storage available for long-distance moves?

Yes. Storage can be part of a long-distance moving plan when delivery timing, home possession, or renovation work requires it.

How is storage priced?

Storage pricing depends on shipment size, duration, handling, packing needs, and whether storage is part of a local or long-distance move.
