Common questions

Do you provide cross-country moving in Canada?

Yes. Purely Canadian Movers helps coordinate cross-country moves across Canada with written estimates, packing, storage, and valuation coverage options.

How much does a cross-country move cost?

Cost depends on route, home size, shipment weight or volume, access, packing, storage, valuation coverage, and timing.

How long does a cross-country move take?

Transit time depends on route schedule, distance, shipment size, season, access, and whether storage is needed.

Can packing and storage be included?

Yes. Packing, storage, unpacking, and valuation coverage options can be added to a cross-country moving estimate.
