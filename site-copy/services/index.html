Common questions

What moving services do you provide?

Purely Canadian Movers provides local moving, long-distance moving, cross-country moving, office moving, packing, storage, valuation coverage, and selected Canada-USA moving services.

Do you provide written estimates?

Yes. Written estimates are prepared based on the details provided, including origin, destination, home size, shipment size, access, packing, storage, and timing.

Do you use moving brokers?

No. Purely Canadian Movers is not a moving broker. Long-distance moves are coordinated through Purely Canadian Movers and the Great Canadian Van Lines agent network.

Can services be combined?

Yes. Packing, storage, valuation coverage, local moving, office moving, and long-distance moving services can be combined when needed.
