Professional packing services for local, long-distance, storage, and office moves

Purely Canadian Movers helps families, condo residents, offices, and long-distance customers plan packing before moving day. Packing support can include cartons, packing paper, tape, fragile-item preparation, wardrobe boxes, storage-ready packing, and valuation coverage options.

Home and condo packing

Packing help for kitchens, dishes, glassware, books, lamps, clothing, electronics, artwork, and general household goods before a local or long-distance move.

Packing for storage

Storage-ready packing helps protect items when possession dates, renovations, or long-distance delivery windows do not line up.

Fragile-item planning

The team can review fragile, high-value, or unusual items before moving day so the right materials and handling plan are included.

Common questions

Do you provide packing services?

Yes. Purely Canadian Movers provides packing services for local moves, long-distance moves, office moves, storage moves, and selected packing-only needs depending on schedule and scope.

Can you provide boxes and supplies?

Yes. Packing supplies can be included with the estimate, including cartons, packing paper, tape, wardrobe boxes, and materials suited to fragile items.

Can packing be combined with storage?

Yes. Packing, storage, moving, and valuation coverage options can be coordinated together when timing or delivery dates require storage.

How much do packing services cost?

Packing cost depends on the number of rooms, box count, fragile items, supplies, crew time, and whether packing is part of a local, office, storage, or long-distance move.
