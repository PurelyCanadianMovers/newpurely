Common questions

Are you a Great Canadian Van Lines agent?

Yes. Purely Canadian Movers works as an authorized agent of Great Canadian Van Lines for long-distance moves across Canada.

Does this mean you are a broker?

No. Purely Canadian Movers is not a moving broker. The move is coordinated through Purely Canadian Movers and the Great Canadian Van Lines agent network.

Why is agent support useful?

Agent support helps coordinate long-distance route planning, linehaul, delivery windows, storage, valuation coverage, and documentation.

Can I ask how my move will be handled?

Yes. Customers should ask about the route, carrier coordination, delivery window, valuation coverage, and any packing or storage needs before booking.
