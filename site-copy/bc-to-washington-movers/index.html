What paperwork is needed for a BC to Washington move?

Requirements vary by situation, but cross-border household moves commonly require photo identification, a clear inventory, and completed customs paperwork such as U.S. Customs Form 3299 where applicable.

Can you move plants, alcohol, or firearms into Washington?

Some items are restricted, inspected, delayed, or require separate paperwork. Plants, soil, fresh food, alcohol, firearms, ammunition, and regulated goods should be discussed before moving day.

How long does customs take?

Customs timing depends on documentation, inspection requirements, route timing, and border conditions. Complete paperwork and a clear inventory help reduce delays.
